The project is an automated lectures, classes planner for a college(for simplicity sake we are going 
with less number of variables involved).

5 sections (5 days a week classes)
3 rooms available

classes
1- Databases
2- OOP
3- Linear Algebra
4- Civics
5- Islamic Studies
6- Digital Logic Design

                                            ____________PLAN____________
Assign  rooms to sections for lectures in way that 
-> Every class has all the required credit hours covered for a week(18 credit hours).
-> Can give breaks to sections incase no room is available, adhering to the above constraint.
